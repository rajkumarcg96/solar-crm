Types of Solar Systems

Apni zaroorat ke hisab se sahi solar system chunein

Most Popular

On-Grid Solar System

Ye system direct electricity grid se juda hota hai. Isme batteries ki zaroorat nahi hoti. Net Metering ke dwara aap extra bijli wapas sarkar ko bech sakte hain.

Best for: Cities & Areas with less power cuts.

With Battery

Off-Grid Solar System

Isme batteries ka use hota hai jo bijli store karti hain. Jab dhoop nahi hoti ya light gol hoti hai, tab battery se power milti hai.

Best for: Villages & Areas with frequent power cuts.

Advanced

Hybrid Solar System

Ye On-Grid aur Off-Grid dono ka combination hai. Ye grid se bhi juda hota hai aur isme battery backup bhi hota hai.

Best for: Industries & Premium Homes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Aapke sawal, hamare jawab

1kW Solar Panel lagwane ka kharch kitna hai?
Solar ka kharch system type (On-grid/Off-grid) aur panel quality (Poly/Mono) par depend karta hai. Mote taur par subsidy ke baad 1kW ka kharch ₹50,000 se ₹70,000 ke beech ho sakta hai. Sahi quote ke liye humein call karein.
Net Metering kya hai?
Net Metering ek billing mechanism hai. Isme aapke solar panel dwara banayi gayi extra bijli grid (sarkar) ko bhej di jati hai. Raat mein jab solar nahi chalta, aap grid se bijli lete hain. Mahine ke ant mein hisab hota hai.
Kya Solar Panels par loan milta hai?
Haan, lagbhag sabhi nationalized banks solar rooftop installation ke liye aasan daro par loan pradan karte hain. PM Surya Ghar yojana ke tahat bhi loan suvidha uplabdh hai.
Solar Panels ki life kitni hoti hai?
Achhi quality ke solar panels ki life 25 saal se adhik hoti hai. Sunskar Solar aapko 25 saal ki performance warranty wale panels hi provide karta hai.
Maintenance (rakh-rakhav) kitna lagta hai?
Solar system mein maintenance na ke barabar hai. Bas mahine mein 1-2 baar panels par jami dhool (dust) ko paani se saaf karna padta hai taaki efficiency bani rahe.
